Shihab Nurun Nabi

Art Director | Production Designer

Film & Television

Bangladesh National Film Award 2021 as Best Art Director
On March 9, 2023, Prime Minister Sheikh Hasina presented the National Film Awards 2021 at the Bangabandhu International Conference Center (BICC) in the city, honoring 35 recipients for their outstanding contributions to the film industry. The ceremony, overseen by Information and Broadcasting Minister Dr. Hasan Mahmud, also featured speeches from Hasanul Haq Inu, chairman of the Parliamentary Standing Committee on Information and Broadcasting, and Information Secretary Humayun Kabir Khandaker. Among the awardees, Shihab Nurun Nabi marked a remarkable achievement in his career by securing the National Film Award-2021 for Best Art Direction. This award, a testament to his exceptional talent and creativity in the realm of art direction, was a significant moment for him. Receiving this honor directly from the Honorable Prime Minister of Bangladesh further underscored the importance of his contribution to the art and film industry.
BABISAS Award-2022 as Best Art Director
Shihab Nurun Nabi, amidst the elegance of the 22nd BABISAS award ceremony at KIB Auditorium, receives the Best Art Director award on 09 September, 2023. This prestigious event, hosted by the Bangladesh Entertainment Journalists Association, saw Babisas president Abul Hossain Majumder overseeing the ceremony, and the Honorable State Minister Dr. Md. Enamur Rahman, MP, as the chief guest, who personally presented the award to a deserving Shihab Nurun Nabi.
Bangladesh Achievers Award-2022 as Best Art Director
On July 24, 2023, the Banquet Hall of Lakeshore Hotel in Dhaka transformed into a venue of splendor and celebration for the Bangladesh Achievers Awards-2023. This esteemed event was a gathering of distinguished personalities and talents from various fields, all coming together to honor the remarkable achievements of individuals who have excelled in their respective domains. Among these illustrious honorees, Shihab Nurun Nabi emerged as a figure of significant recognition, being bestowed with the award for Best Art Director. His achievement in this category highlighted his exceptional skills and contributions to the field of art direction, setting him apart as a leading figure in his profession. The award ceremony was not only a testament to Shihab Nurun Nabi's artistic prowess but also a reflection of the vibrant and dynamic nature of the creative industry in Bangladesh.
