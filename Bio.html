Bio

In the realm of cinema, where imagination meets visual expression, Shihab Nurun Nabi stands as a luminary, weaving tales with his artistic brilliance. Widely recognized as one of the most promising Art Directors of his generation, Shihab has carved a niche for himself through a diverse portfolio that transcends conventional boundaries.
At the heart of Shihab's acclaim is his role as the Art Director for the award-winning film "Nonajoler Kabbo" ("The Salt in Our Waters"). Set against the backdrop of a remote coastal village in Bangladesh, the film unfolds a clash of cultures intertwined with stories of love and resilience. The 46th Bangladesh National Film Award crowned Shihab as the Best Art Director for his exceptional contribution to this cinematic masterpiece, which also clinched multiple categories, including Best Film and Best Art Direction.
Shihab's artistic journey extends beyond the silver screen, delving into the world of TV commercials. Over the years, he has lent his creative vision to more than 800 memorable commercials, becoming the driving force behind campaigns for international giants like Unilever, Huawei, and Samsung, as well as national brands like Grameenphone and Banglalink.
The digital frontier also bears the imprint of Shihab's creativity. His role as the Art Director for the Hoichoi OTT platform series "Mohanagar-2" catapulted it into the realm of highest-viewed web series in the Bengali language. His anthology film contributions, such as "Ei Muhurte" and "Youtumer," delve into contemporary issues with a unique artistic perspective, showcasing the convergence of traditional storytelling and modern digital narratives.
Beyond the spotlight of the entertainment industry, Shihab Nurun Nabi is a business visionary. As a Director and Partner at Studio Nine and Half, he has played a pivotal role in establishing a state-of-the-art filming stage facility, becoming the epicenter for film and TV production in Bangladesh.
Shihab's artistic journey isn't confined to the cinematic realm alone. A seasoned artist, he has left his mark in various art exhibitions and competitions, showcasing the depth of his creative prowess. As a General Member of the Bangladesh Art Director Association, Shihab actively contributes to the artistic community's growth and development.
In a career that spans TV commercials, gripping fictions, and impactful films, Shihab Nurun Nabi emerges as a visionary, shaping the visual narrative of his generation. His journey is not just a chronicle of artistic achievements but a testament to the transformative power of storytelling and visual aesthetics.
